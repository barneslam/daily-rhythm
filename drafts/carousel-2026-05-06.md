# Revenue Math: What Actually Drives Growth

## Slide 1: The Question
You have:
- 5 salespeople
- Good product
- 200 leads/month

Why isn't revenue growing 40% YoY?

## Slide 2: Most Teams Blame
❌ "Not enough leads"
❌ "Sales team isn't hungry"
❌ "Market is slowing"

## Slide 3: Usually It's
✓ Wrong ICP (chasing deals that don't fit)
✓ Broken motion (long, expensive sales cycles)
✓ No weekly accountability (nobody owns the pipeline)

## Slide 4: The Math
- Current conversion: 5% (200 leads → 10 customers)
- Fix motion: 8% possible (200 leads → 16 customers)
- **60% pipeline growth from execution, not hiring**

## Slide 5: What Good Looks Like
- Weekly pipeline review (not monthly)
- Defined deal stages (not "it's in sales")
- Velocity targets (not just activity counts)

## Slide 6: Your Next Move
One week audit:
- Map your real pipeline conversion
- Find the bottleneck
- Fix it before hiring
