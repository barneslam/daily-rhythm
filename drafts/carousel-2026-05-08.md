# The Weekly Rhythm That Drives Revenue

## Slide 1: What Kills Most Plans
You have a quarterly plan.
By week 2, nobody's following it.

Why?

## Slide 2: Plans Fail Because
- Too abstract ("grow 40%")
- Too infrequent (quarterly, not weekly)
- No daily execution layer
- Winners and losers aren't named

## Slide 3: What Actually Works
**Weekly sprint on revenue:**
- Monday: Confirm pipeline + targets
- Wed: Midweek pulse (adjust if needed)
- Fri: Review, plan next week

## Slide 4: Monday Meeting (30 min)
- Pipeline review (stage by stage)
- Identify stuck deals
- Assign owners + actions
- Confirm Friday win target

## Slide 5: Wednesday Pulse (15 min)
- Quick status (on track? behind?)
- Remove blockers (pricing, tech, approval)
- Adjust if needed

## Slide 6: Friday Review (30 min)
- Won/lost deals (what happened?)
- Velocity vs plan
- Next week's forecast
- Celebrate wins + fix issues
