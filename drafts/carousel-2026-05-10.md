# The Signal Most Founders Miss

## Slide 1: The Silence That Costs You
You hit $5M ARR.
Growth slows.
Everyone says "hire more salespeople."

Wrong answer.

## Slide 2: The Real Problem
Your market sent you signals.
Your ICP shifted.
Your message didn't.

Now your reps are selling to the wrong buyer
with the wrong story.

## Slide 3: What Bad Signal Looks Like
- Long sales cycles (80+ days)
- High discounting to close
- Churning customers in month 3-4
- Reps saying "leads aren't quality"

## Slide 4: What Good Signal Looks Like
✓ Deals close in 30-45 days
✓ Customers renew without being asked
✓ One clear reason why they bought
✓ Reps can explain the win in one sentence

## Slide 5: The Fix
1. Interview your last 10 closed-won deals
2. Find the single pattern that drove the close
3. Rebuild your message around that pattern
4. Run it for 30 days before hiring anyone
