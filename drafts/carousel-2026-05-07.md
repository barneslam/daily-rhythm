# Why Execution Beats Hiring

## Slide 1: Common Decision
Growth stalled.
Decision: "Hire more sales reps."
Budget: $500K.

## Slide 2: The Problem
- Most founders don't have a working sales motion
- Hiring into broken execution = expensive failure
- New reps follow old (broken) playbook

## Slide 3: Better Path
Before hiring:
✓ Fix your positioning (are you targeting the right buyer?)
✓ Fix your motion (is your sales cycle reasonable?)
✓ Build your rhythm (can your team execute daily?)

## Slide 4: The Numbers
- Broken motion + 1 rep = $0 extra revenue
- Fixed motion + same rep = $50-100K ARR

**It's not about headcount. It's about system.**

## Slide 5: Real Example
Client had:
- 8 AEs (expensive)
- 3% conversion
- After fixing motion: 3 AEs, 12% conversion
- **Result: +$2M ARR, -$400K payroll**

## Slide 6: Your Audit
- How long is your sales cycle? (should be <60 days)
- What's your real win rate? (should be 5-10% at least)
- Who owns weekly accountability?

---
**Stop hiring. Start executing.**
