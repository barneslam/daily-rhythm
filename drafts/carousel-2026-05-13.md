# Why Your GTM Breaks (Before You Hire)

## Slide 1: The Moment It Starts
Your offer is solid.
Your team believes in it.
Market is real.

Then execution stalls.

## Slide 2: It's Not What You Think
You blame:
- Bad leads
- Wrong pricing
- Sales team isn't hungry enough

You're looking at symptoms, not the root.

## Slide 3: The Real Problem
No clear daily rhythm.

Nobody knows what priority wins the day.
Nobody knows who decides at 3pm.
Nobody has a signal if things break.

## Slide 4: What Breaks First
Pipeline visibility disappears.
You can't tell if week 2 is better than week 1.
Activity ≠ results.

## Slide 5: Then The Team Fractures
Sales doesn't trust the leads.
Marketing doesn't know what sales needs.
Leadership guesses instead of deciding.

Everyone's working hard.
Nobody's aligned on what matters.

## Slide 6: Revenue Stalls
But it feels like a headcount problem.

So you hire.

## Slide 7: The Trap
You hire a new VP Sales.
Or a CMO.
Or both.

They bring energy. Different methods. New tools.

But they still don't have the rhythm the organization needs.

## Slide 8: Now You Have Two Problems
Same broken system.
More expensive.
More frustrated.

## Slide 9: The Real Question
Before you hire anyone else, ask:

Do we have clarity on daily execution?
Can everyone see the same pipeline?
Do we make decisions in real time?

## Slide 10: The Fix Starts Here
Not with hiring.

With structure.
