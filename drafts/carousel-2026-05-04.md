# The Revenue Execution Gap

## Slide 1: The Problem
Most teams have strategy.
Few execute it consistently.

The gap? **Execution architecture.**

## Slide 2: What Gets Lost
- Strategy is clear
- Sales is hired
- But the **daily rhythm** falls apart
- Pipeline feels random
- Revenue plateaus

## Slide 3: Why It Happens
Strategy = Board room
Execution = Daily standups

The translation layer breaks.

## Slide 4: The Fix
- Define the GTM operating model
- Build the rhythm (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Measure what matters
- Adjust weekly (not yearly)

## Slide 5: Result
Same team.
Better execution.
Predictable revenue.
