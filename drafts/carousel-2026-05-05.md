# How CROs Actually Get Built

## Slide 1: The Myth
"Hire a VP Sales and watch revenue scale."

## Slide 2: The Reality
A VP Sales + broken execution architecture = expensive hire that fails.

## Slide 3: What Really Works
**CRO = Chief Revenue Officer**
Not just "best salesperson."

The role: Design the operating system.

## Slide 4: 3 Pillars
1. **ICP Clarity** — Who you actually win
2. **Motion Clarity** — How they buy from you
3. **Execution Rhythm** — Daily, weekly, monthly practices

## Slide 5: The Difference
Broken org: VP Sales works harder
Smart org: Everyone works smarter

## Slide 6: Your CRO's First 90 Days
- Week 1-2: Map current state
- Week 3-4: Define ICP + motion
- Week 5-12: Build daily rhythm + teams
