# Visual Guidelines: 48-Hour Recovery Sprint Content

## MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS
- ✅ **REAL PEOPLE ONLY** — No AI, no avatars, no fake people
- ✅ **Business context** — Actual work setting, not posed
- ✅ **High quality** — Minimum 1200x630px for social
- ✅ **Text overlay safe** — Don't cover faces or key elements
- ✅ **Authentic tone** — Candid, not overly styled

---

## PHOTO SOURCING (FREE)

### Best Sources:
1. **Unsplash** (unsplash.com)
   - Search: "founder laptop", "ceo desk", "consultant notes", "business proposal", "email inbox"
   - Filter: High resolution, realistic

2. **Pexels** (pexels.com)
   - Search: "business people", "office work", "entrepreneur", "proposal", "notes"

3. **Pixabay** (pixabay.com)
   - Search: "small business owner", "remote work", "business meeting", "review"

### DO NOT USE:
- ❌ Stock photos with generic smiling poses
- ❌ AI-generated images
- ❌ Cartoons or avatars
- ❌ Abstract illustrations
- ❌ Photos with fake text or logos

## PHOTO ASSIGNMENTS (By Post)

### Monday: Opportunity Leakage
**Vibe:** Founder reviewing inbox, slightly frustrated, real moment  
**Search:** "founder laptop", "reading email", "inbox", "thinking"  
**Why:** Reflects the pain of old conversations going quiet

### Tuesday: Wasted Lead Spend
**Vibe:** Small business owner at desk with notes/spreadsheet  
**Search:** "ceo desk", "business notes", "financial planning", "analytics"  
**Why:** Shows the cost of inefficiency

### Wednesday: Stalled Proposal Recovery
**Vibe:** Person reviewing documents, focused, reviewing proposal  
**Search:** "review document", "proposal review", "business contract", "agreement"  
**Why:** Direct visual tie to proposals

### Thursday: Warm Referrals
**Vibe:** Two people in conversation (Zoom call or office setting)  
**Search:** "business meeting", "zoom call", "conversation", "team discussion"  
**Why:** Shows the collaborative nature of referrals

### Friday: Follow-Up Rhythm
**Vibe:** Calendar, planner, or person organizing tasks  
**Search:** "calendar", "planner", "schedule", "organization", "productivity"  
**Why:** Represents the system/rhythm

### Saturday: Case Study
**Vibe:** Person on laptop, positive engagement, working  
**Search:** "working", "laptop", "focused", "productive", "engaged"  
**Why:** Shows momentum and progress

### Sunday: Founder Reflection
**Vibe:** Founder alone, thoughtful, honest moment  
**Search:** "founder thinking", "reflection", "alone at desk", "introspection"  
**Why:** Matches the personal, honest tone

## TEXT OVERLAY RULES

✅ DO:
- Place text in bottom third of image
- Keep faces completely visible
- Use high-contrast text (white on dark, dark on light)
- Maximum 25% of image should be covered by text
- Use sans-serif font (Arial, Helvetica, or similar)

❌ DON'T:
- Cover faces
- Overlay text on busy backgrounds
- Use thin fonts that don't stand out
- Add too many text blocks
- Use decorative fonts

---

## COLOR PALETTE

**Accent colors for text overlays:**
- Primary: #a07828 (gold)
- Secondary: #1a1a1a (dark)
- Highlight: #4a7c59 (green)
- Neutral: White or black for contrast
