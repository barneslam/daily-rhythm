# The First 10 Targets: How We Find Them

Cold outreach works when you target people already showing buying signals.

**Three signals we're scanning for today:**

1. **Hiring**
   - LinkedIn update: "Excited to announce we're hiring 5 sales reps"
   - Signal: They've hit a revenue threshold where scaling becomes urgent
   - Urgency: High — they need help NOW

2. **Product Launch**
   - Job boards + company announcements: "We just launched X"
   - Signal: They're about to do customer acquisition
   - Urgency: High — timing matters

3. **Public Plateau**
   - Press coverage or CEO interview mentioning "growth challenges"
   - Signal: They're acknowledging a problem publicly
   - Urgency: High — they're thinking about solutions

## The Outreach Hook

Don't lead with your offer. Lead with the signal:

*"Saw you just hired your first sales team. I work with SaaS companies on scaling go-to-market in the first 90 days. Worth a 15-min call?"*

**What happened:** 
- You showed you researched them (credibility)
- You referenced their signal (relevance)
- You offered a specific outcome (clarity)
- You gave them an easy yes/no (low friction)

## This Week

Response rate tells you whether your offer is resonating or your targeting is off.
