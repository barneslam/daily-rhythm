# Conor Deployment Brief — Week 1

## The Offer (What We're Selling)

**GTM Breakdown Session** — 90-minute 1-on-1 diagnostic for solo founders and small-team operators who have a viable offer but are stuck in execution.

**Price:** $750–$1,500 depending on complexity  
**Format:** Single session, no retainer, no 12-week program  
**Outcome:** Execution plan + daily operating rhythm + the 3 things actually blocking revenue

## Why This Matters

The gap between *knowing* what to do and *actually doing it* is where revenue dies. Most founders don't fail from lack of ideas — they fail from lack of rhythm. This session fixes that.

## Conor's Role This Week

**Status:** Briefing only (deployment likely Week 4 when first paid sessions are closing)

### What you need to know:
1. **The ICP:** Solo operators, small-team founders, SaaS founders, e-commerce operators, service providers  
   - They have a product/service that works  
   - They're stuck on execution (inconsistent prospecting, poor conversion, weak sales rhythm)  
   - They usually think the problem is their offer, but it's their operating system

2. **The positioning angle:**  
   - "I've built the operating rhythm that got me out of this trap. I run this diagnostic for other operators."  
   - NOT: "Here's my 12-week coaching program"  
   - YES: "90 minutes, you walk out with the system, you run it yourself"

3. **What you do NOT do:**  
   - Don't oversell — this is a diagnostic, not transformation  
   - Don't mention retainers or ongoing coaching (not part of this offer)  
   - Don't position as therapy or motivation — position as operator-to-operator rhythm debugging

## Your Deployment Script (Week 4+)

When a warm lead agrees to a call, Conor deploys on the *close conversation* (not discovery).

**Your 3 talking points:**

1. **Credibility:** "I've been where you are. Built a company from idea to exit. I know the gap between knowing and doing."
2. **Proof:** "The daily operating rhythm I ran is now what I diagnose for founders in your situation."
3. **Investment perspective:** "This isn't a coaching program. It's a 90-minute system you own and run yourself. No dependency on me."

## What Success Looks Like

- Lead says "Yes, let's do a session" after hearing Conor's 3-minute credibility drop
- Lead doesn't ask about ongoing coaching (because Conor made clear it's single-session)
- Lead gets on the calendar for Week 4+ (first paid sessions)
