## Research Insights & Recommendations

### Challenge with Target: Web search heavily surfaces unicorns (>$100M ARR)
The challenge in this trigger scan is that web search and public data predominantly surface late-stage/mega-company operators. The ICP sweet spot ($2M-$50M revenue, execution-stalled founders) typically:

- Have lower public profiles
- Are not covered by TechCrunch, VentureBeat, or major publications
- Require database access (Crunchbase filters, Growth List, Carta) to identify systematically
- Are scattered across YC batches, private funding rounds, and niche industry verticals

### Confirmed Trigger Patterns Across Research

**Trigger 1 (Hiring Sales):** Companies in $2M-$50M range actively hiring VP Sales typically:
- Post 12-18 months post-Series A funding
- Show NRR plateau (self-serve exhausted)
- Begin upmarket expansion (need sales teams)

**Trigger 3 (Plateau):** Growth ceiling markers validated across research:
- SaaS NRR compressed to 101% median (Jan 2026)
- Median EV/Revenue at 3.4x (March 2026)
- AI disruption reducing seat-based growth (10 AI agents = 100 seats no longer needed)
- Execution challenges blamed on misalignment, junior team, and weak positioning

**Trigger 5 (Funding):** Series A/B companies show 1-2 week window for reaching out post-announcement (confirmed via SaaS Rise research)
